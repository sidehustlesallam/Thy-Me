Thy-me — Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

At Thy-me, we believe your personal memories should remain yours. This Privacy Policy describes how your information is collected, processed, and stored when you use the Thy-me mobile application.

Our core philosophy is simple: We only collect and share the absolute minimum amount of data required to print and deliver your physical postcards. We do not track your location, we do not build advertising profiles, and we never sell your information.

1. Information Stored Locally on Your Device

To make sending postcards as seamless as possible, certain data is stored directly in your device’s secure local storage sandbox. This data stays on your phone and is never accessed by us unless a transaction is explicitly initiated.

  • Your Photo Captures: Photos captured within the app’s 3:2 viewfinder are processed locally on your device to compile your layout.
  • The Address Book: Physical names and delivery addresses you input are saved locally on your device so you can instantly select them for future cards without re-typing them.
  • Anonymous Session Tokens: The application uses automated secure identification tokens stored on your device to securely keep track of your drafts and order history without forcing you to make an account.

2. Information Shared with Third-Party Partners (And Why)

Thy-me does not own printing presses or mail delivery trucks. Therefore, when you tap "Send Card," we must securely pass specific transaction details to our trusted fulfillment partners to physically manufacture and deliver your order.

We only share data with the following categories of service providers:

A. Print Fulfillment Infrastructure Partners

  • What we share: Your compiled postcard layout design file (image and message text) along with the recipient’s physical name and shipping address.
  • Why we share it: This data is strictly required so our commercial printing partners can physically print the high-resolution photo on card stock, apply postage, and hand it over to Royal Mail for final delivery to the letterbox.

B. Secure Payment Processing Gateways

  • What we share: Payment transaction values and card verification data.
  • Why we share it: To process your single-tap checkout securely. Thy-me never sees, stores, or handles your raw credit card numbers or Google Pay credentials; all billing transactions are handled entirely by fully compliant, secure industry-standard payment networks.

3. Data Retention Boundaries

  • Local Data: You remain in full control of your local data. Deleting the Thy-me application from your smartphone immediately and permanently wipes your local address book, draft images, and cached session history.
  • Fulfillment Data: Our manufacturing partners only retain your image and address data for a short, temporary operational window (typically 7 to 30 days) solely to handle print quality audits or delivery troubleshooting, after which it is automatically scheduled for permanent deletion.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please contact us at support@lexsfolks.com.
